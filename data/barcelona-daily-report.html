This report is updated automatically each day to highlight key insights from the Mosquito Alert Barcelona platform. It focuses on the 7-day-ahead forecast of the Vector Risk Index (VRI) for tiger mosquitoes (Aedes albopictus) in Barcelona. This provides a relative measure of the variation in the risk of human contact with tiger mosquitoes across Barcelona and over time. The 7-day-ahead forecast (which starts on the current day) is based on the weather forecast provided daily by the Meteorological Service of Catalonia. The VRI forecast is updated between 11:30 am and 12:30 pm each day.
